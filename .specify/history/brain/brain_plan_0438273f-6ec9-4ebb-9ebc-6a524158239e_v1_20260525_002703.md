## Proposed Workflows & Steps

We will build the following workflows using the standard step schemas:

### 1. `polymarket_clob_order_routing`
- **Domain**: `finance`
- **Tags**: `[polymarket, orders, clob, execution]`
- **Steps**:
  - **Step 0**: `emerald-exchange` (Initialize CLOB Client & API key signatures)
  - **Step 1**: `mcp_market_data` (Fetch current order book depth and spreads)
  - **Step 2**: `mcp_orders` (Construct and cryptographically sign a limit/market order)
  - **Step 3**: `emerald-exchange` (Submit order payload to Polymarket API endpoint)
  - **Step 4**: `mcp_portfolio` (Query active position ledger to verify fill status)

### 2. `polymarket_whale_copy_trading`
- **Domain**: `finance`
- **Tags**: `[polymarket, copy-trading, supabase, tracking]`
- **Steps**:
  - **Step 0**: `mcp_signals` (Poll designated top trader wallet addresses or transaction streams)
  - **Step 1**: `database-tools` (Sync trade activity to local Supabase database to avoid double-execution)
  - **Step 2**: `mcp_risk` (Validate mirror trades against local capital constraints & limits)
  - **Step 3**: `mcp_orders` (Mirror trades in real-time by generating buy/sell orders via CLOB client)
  - **Step 4**: `database-tools` (Update local ledger with executing price and order status)

### 3. `polymarket_multi_strategy_arb`
- **Domain**: `finance`
- **Tags**: `[polymarket, arbitrage, strategy, sentiment]`
- **Steps**:
  - **Step 0**: `mcp_market_data` (Fetch odds from Polymarket target prediction contract)
  - **Step 1**: `web-search` (Scrape sentiment signals from Twitter and Google News for current event probability)
  - **Step 2**: `mcp_signals` (Poll external prediction providers or sports betting odds to identify pricing discrepancies)
  - **Step 3**: `mcp_strategy` (Assess arbitrage viability and select the highest expected value bet)
  - **Step 4**: `mcp_orders` (Route execution buy orders to capture pricing arbitrage)

### 4. `polymarket_btc_15m_scheduler`
- **Domain**: `finance`
- **Tags**: `[polymarket, btc, scheduler, grafana, redis]`
- **Steps**:
  - **Step 0**: `mcp_market_data` (Retrieve historical 15m price candles for BTC/USD)
  - **Step 1**: `mcp_signals` (Run technical indicator scripts like RSI, MACD, and EMA)
  - **Step 2**: `mcp_strategy` (Execute the 7-phase predicting up/down trend algorithm)
  - **Step 3**: `mcp_risk` (Validate dynamic trailing stop-loss values based on current market volatility)
  - **Step 4**: `mcp_orders` (Submit prediction bets to the corresponding 15m market via CLOB)
  - **Step 5**: `infrastructure-orchestrator` (Send logs and performance stats to Redis and Grafana dashboard)

### 5. `polymarket_liquidity_mm`
- **Domain**: `finance`
- **Tags**: `[polymarket, market-making, liquidity, delta-hedging]`
- **Steps**:
  - **Step 0**: `mcp_market_data` (Monitor target orderbook bid-ask spread and volume depth)
  - **Step 1**: `mcp_portfolio` (Analyze current inventory weight to determine balance ratio)
  - **Step 2**: `mcp_risk` (Calculate quoting offsets: skew buy side if short, ask side if long)
  - **Step 3**: `mcp_orders` (Place dual-sided limit orders matching inventory limits to capture the spread)
  - **Step 4**: `mcp_orders` (Cancel stale/out-of-range orders and dynamically update bid-ask offsets)
